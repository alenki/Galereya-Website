    About GALEREYA

    TRC GALEREYA is a regional shopping and entertainment complex, which is a modern facility for trade and leisure for the population with average and above-average income levels.
    • The concept of the GALEREYA complex provides for a unified style of design for the entire interior space and an individual embodiment of each sales department, providing customers with the opportunity to make purchases, choosing from well-known brands of leading manufacturers of clothing, footwear and accessories. A comfortable atmosphere, the presence of restaurants and cafes, a cinema, bowling, a children's playground, a spacious parking area, a high-quality ventilation and air conditioning system, elevators and escalators contribute to a pleasant time in the GALEREYA.
